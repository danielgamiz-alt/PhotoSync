PhotoSync

Back up your phone's photos and videos to your family's own computer — free, private, no cloud subscription.

Download for Android
Android 8.0 or newer · free
Or scan with your phone's camera

Installing it (about a minute)

1

Download & open

Tap the button above, then open the downloaded .apk file.

2

Allow the install

Android will say it's from an "unknown source" — this is normal for apps outside the Play Store. Tap Settings, turn on Allow from this source, then go back and tap Install.

3

If you see a warning

If Play Protect shows "unsafe app", tap More details → Install anyway. (It flags anything not from the Play Store.)

4

Open & connect

Open PhotoSync, allow photo access, enter your name, and tap Find server while on your home Wi‑Fi. Done — your photos start backing up.

One thing to set up first

PhotoSync uploads to PhotoServer — a small free program that runs on the household's PC or Raspberry Pi and keeps the photos on your own drive. Set that up once by following the instructions on GitHub. Your phone only backs up when it's on the same Wi‑Fi and that computer is on.

Free & open source · github.com/danielgamiz-alt/PhotoServer
